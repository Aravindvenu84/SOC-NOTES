# a. Popular SOC Ticketing Tools

## ServiceNow: Enterprise ITSM platform; incident, problem, change management; integrates with SOC tools.

## Jira: Issue tracking and project management; customizable workflows; supports incident tracking.

## TheHive: Open-source SOC platform; incident creation, management, correlation; integrates with threat intelligence.

# b. How Ticketing Systems Help SOC Operations

## Track Incidents: Log every alert with source, severity, analyst assignment.

## Escalate Incidents: Automatically or manually escalate based on severity/SLA/workflow rules.

## Close Incidents: Document root cause, mitigation steps, and resolution; maintain historical record.

## Summary: Essential for structured incident handling, faster response, accountability, and SOC metrics.
